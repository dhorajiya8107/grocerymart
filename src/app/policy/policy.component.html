Introduction

    Thank you for choosing The Grocery Mart. We appreciate your business and want to ensure a seamless shopping experience. This Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which you may cancel your order.

    Order Cancellation

    1. Before Order Processing: You may cancel your order at any time before it is processed. Once the order is confirmed and processed, it cannot be canceled.

    2. Order Modification: While we strive to accommodate order changes, we cannot guarantee modifications after the order is processed. You may contact our customer service for assistance.

    Cancellation and Refund Policy

    1. Prepaid Orders: If you cancel your order before it is processed, a full refund will be issued to the original payment method within 4 to 5 business days.

    2. Partially Shipped Orders: If part of your order has been shipped, you may request a cancellation for the remaining items. The items already shipped cannot be canceled.

    3. Out of Stock Items: If an item is out of stock, we will notify you and offer a replacement or a refund for that item.

    Exceptions to Cancellation Policy

    1. Perishable Items: Orders containing perishable items, such as fresh produce, meat, or dairy products, cannot be canceled once processed.

    2. Subscription Boxes: Cancellations for subscription boxes must be made before the renewal date to avoid charges for the next box.

    How to Cancel Your Order

    To cancel your order, please contact our customer service at 1800-1800-1800. You will need to provide your order number for verification.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions or require further clarification regarding our cancellation policy, please contact our customer service department at 1800-1800-1800.

    Important Notice

    This cancellation policy is subject to change without prior notice. Please check our website regularly for updates.
